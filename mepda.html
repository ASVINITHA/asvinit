MOBILE ESTATE PLANNING FOR DIGITAL ASSETS (MEPDA)
Problem Statement
Many People own online accounts, with some having financial values like Internet banking, e-wallet and cryptocurrency wallets. In the case of sudden death, their heirs are unware of Digital assets possessed by the deceased person, which causes the assests to be lost forever,and the heirs might not receive the assets. If an estate plan did not account for digital assets properly, the beneficiaries would not be able to access them.
Project Description
Mobile Estate Planning for Digital Assets (MEPDA); a mobile application that can help asset owners to store their digital asset information in one place and assign their corresponding heirs. All digital assets will be stored and accessible to the asset planner (administrator) in a single application for future reference or clarification. Moreover, the asset owners also need to answer the monthly quiz as a life monitoring approach to ensure the owners are in good health and alive
