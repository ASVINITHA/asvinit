SISTEM PENGURUSAN TEMUDUGA PERUMAHAN
Problem Statement
The Housing Department at Wisma Darul Aman use a manual paper-based process to record and manage customer information during interviews. This inefficient method involves extensive handwriting, time-consuming calculations, and difficulty in accessing customer data when needed. The staff faces challenges in managing customers effectively due to this outdated process.
Project Description
The Housing Interview Management System is a user-friendly web-based application developed in Malay language for the Housing Department at Wisma Darul Aman. It replaces the manual paper-based process by allowing staff to digitally record applicant information during interviews. The system enables staff to input applicant responses, assign scores, and retrieve information easily. This streamlines the interview process, improves efficiency, reduces paper usage, and simplifies data management for Rumah PPR applicants.
