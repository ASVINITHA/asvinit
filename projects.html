Projects

(1) PROTOTYPE

I developed a prototype specifically for my client's online business assignment, focusing on Busy Bee Laundry Service. The application I designed provides customers with easy access to the business profile and enables effortless online ordering. Customers can select laundry services, provide instructions and preferences, and schedule convenient pickup times. Using Marvel software, I created an intuitive and visually appealing interface that showcases the business information and simplifies the ordering process. The prototype emphasizes simplicity and user-friendliness, allowing customers to navigate the application, access details, and place orders quickly. View More...

(2) WEB DEVELOPMENT

I created a web-based project for my client's final year project. The project aims to help researchers conduct diary studies online. Traditionally, diary studies were done using paper diaries, which were time-consuming to manage and transcribe. With our web-based system, researchers can easily design, conduct, and analyze diary studies online, saving time and resources. View More...

(3) WEB DEVELOPMENT

I developed a website during my internship with the Housing Department, Secretary of Kedah. It's a specialized system for storing customer information obtained during interviews and calculating scores based on their answers. Exclusively used by the Housing Division at Wisma Darul Aman, staff can input and store customer details, while the system assigns scores automatically. It securely stores all customer information for future reference. View More...

(5) APP DEVELOPMENT

Additionally, there is a monthly quiz feature to monitor the owner's well-being and confirm their status. View More...
