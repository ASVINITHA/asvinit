E-UNIVERSITY RANKING SYSTEM
Scope
The E-University Ranking system is designed to assist students in making informed decisions about local universities. By comparing various aspects such as research output, student/faculty ratio, overall student scores, number of international students, location, and university rankings, this system provides rankings for each university. Through the E-University Ranking System website, students can easily access information about university performance and choose the most suitable institution for their desired course.
System Overview
With an increasing number of students seeking admission to universities each year, making informed decisions about institutions has become challenging. To address this issue, the E-University Ranking System has been developed. This system aims to compare the performance of different universities and provide rankings to aid students in their decision-making process. The rankings of universities will be regularly updated on an annual basis, ensuring that a broader range of institutions are included in the evaluation. By utilizing this system, students can access valuable insights into university performance and make more informed choices regarding their educational journeys.
