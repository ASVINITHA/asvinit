DECISIVE DIARY STUDY
Project Description
Decisive Diary Study is designed to provide researchers with a tool to conduct diary studies online. Diary studies are a qualitative research method that allows researchers to collect rich and detailed data about participants' experiences over an extended period of time. Traditionally, diary studies were conducted using paper-based diaries or journals, which were time-consuming to administer and transcribe. With our web-based diary study system, researchers can easily design,conduct, and analyze diary studies online, saving time and resources. This website is user friendly, secure and customizable to meet the specific needs of each research project.
